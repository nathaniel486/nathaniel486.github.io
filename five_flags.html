Delaware's Five Flags

Delaware has flown under 5 different flags in it's history based on what country had power over the land back in the colonial days. The Five Flags are:


The Netherlands

Source: http://www.mapsofworld.com/images/world-countries-flags/netherlands-flag.gif

Delaware was settled in 1651 by the Dutch West India Company and was a colony of the Netherlands named Fort Casimir


Sweden

Source: https://upload.wikimedia.org/
wikipedia/en/4/4c/Flag_of_Sweden.svg

In 1654 Sweden seized the land and renamed the land to Fort Trinity becuase it was seized on Trinity Sunday.


The Netherlands (again)

Source: http://www.mapsofworld.com/images/world-countries-flags/netherlands-flag.gif

However the Dutch came back the following summer and conqured all of the New Sweden Colony and renamed the area again to Nieuw-Amstel (New Amstel).


Great Britain

Source: https://flagspot.net/images/g/gb.gif

In 1664 the English came and conqured the entire New Netherlands Colony and changed the name of the town for the last time to New Castle.


United States of America

http://feelgrafix.com/data/american-flag/american-flag-7.jpg

On June 15, 1776 Delaware declared it's independence from English rule and became the First State in the United States of America.
